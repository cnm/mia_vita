About RGraph

Started in August 2008 by Richard Heyes RGraph has been going from strength to strength and is now one of the top charting libraries that is available for the HTML5 platform. With the supported number of chart types having grown to over twenty RGraph has a wide appeal and can represent many types of data quickly and efficiently.

RGraph is suitable for all websites with charts being rendered in the latest standards based HTML5 for modern browsers and in VML in the case of IE7 & 8. The size of the Javascript files is small and can be further reduced with minification and compression and so offers significant speed boosts to a website.

An important aspect of the RGraph experience is Customer satisfaction. This can be evidenced in the testimonials that RGraph users have submitted, which can be seen on the front page of the website.

About the founder

An avid snow-boarder and extreme sports enthusiast, Richard has been in the technology field for over 16 years, and in the web development field specifically for 12 of those. The technical expertise that has resulted from working on high performance websites (eg lovefilm.com, adtrader.com) and open source projects lends itself well to producing high quality software that performs extremely well and is easily usable.
